Lewis Hamilton

  • He is a British racing driver, 38 years old
  • Started F1 in 2007 Australian Grand Prix with Mclaren
  • His first win was in 2007 Canadian Grand Prix
  • He moved to Mercedes in 2013 and still with them
  • He has won 7 World Championships, among other records
